B) Cookies

Cookies are small text files which are placed in your browser history and which are used to reconstruct settings and other changes you have already made on your next visit. These functional cookies ensure that our website functions properly. These cookies are stored for a maximum of 2 years, after which they are automatically deleted. For example, the following functions are possible with these cookies:

  1. saving products that you have placed in the shopping basket or added to the wish list
  2. Saving the entries you made during check-out or during an order so that you do not have to enter this data again
  3. Saving default settings such as language, location, number of search results, etc.
  4. Saving settings for an optimal video display, e.g. the desired buffer size and the resolution data of your screen
  5. recording your browser settings in order to optimise the display of our website on your screen
  6. recognising misuse of our websites and services, e.g. by registering several consecutive and failed login attempts
  7. loading the website evenly so that the website remains accessible
  8. saving your login data so that you do not have to re-enter it each time

You have the option of blocking the setting of these cookies and deleting cookies that have already been set. You can obtain more information on this from the manufacturer or in the help function of your Internet browser. However, we must point out that certain functionalities on our website will no longer be available or will only be available to a limited extent if you do not allow these functional cookies.
