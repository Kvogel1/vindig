Our Partners

BKRSCLB RECORDS

https://www.bkrsclb.com/

BKRSCLB is a label founded by rapper Raz Fresco from Toronto, Canada. He created the brand in order to release his instrumental music separate from his as the rap artist, but has since featured beats by several amazing producers as well. Since June 2024 BKRSCLB is officially a sublabel of VINDIG.

Future of Lofi

Spotify Profile

Future of Lofi is our innovative sublabel dedicated to exploring the next evolution of Lo-Fi music. Here, we embrace experimentation and creativity, releasing tracks with artists that push the boundaries of the genre and envision what Lo-Fi will sound like in the years to come. Welcome to the future!

wvvy

https://linktr.ee/wvvy

wvvy is your destination for the finest selection of Lo-Fi house, smooth house and techhouse. Our sublabel is dedicated to curating high-quality tracks that embody positivity and subtlety. Check out the wvvy playlist on Spotify to find soothing rhythms and uplifting vibes.

POSTPARTUM.

https://postpartum-records.com/

POSTPARTUM. is a german underground hip hop label founded in 2016 in Auerbach, Germany. They regularly release cassetttes and vinyl records in small editions, so you can certainly find some rare gems on their page.

Keller Flavour

https://www.instagram.com/kellerflavour/

Keller Flavour is a record label based in Munich, Germany and was founded in 2014. "Hip Hop, Rap, instrumental beat stuff and other things called music is released on limited tapes and vinyl."

Tapefabrik

https://www.tapefabrik.de/

We are one of the official main sponsors of the Tapefabrik Festival! A local underground hip hop festival with several stages (one of them being a VINDIG beat stage), sales stands (dig through some Vinyl Digital records) and cool streetfood options.

BACKSPIN

https://backspin-shop.de/

Backspin is a staple in the german hip hop culture. They have shaped the scene with their magazines, interviews and documentations with and about big names in the industry. We are proud to say that we sponsored their 30 years anniversary this year and are looking forward to many more to come!

Hip Hop Kitchen

https://hiphopkitchen.de/

We have been a partner of the HIPHOP KITCHEN event for several years now and we love it! Enjoy a three course meal prepared by professional chefs, live music by local beatmakers and fun games with the chance to win some vinyls from us.
